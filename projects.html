About

Final project. It will be a mood lamp x motivation quote. The deisgn of the product is in the shape of a star. The side of the lamp will be made of translucent material so that it will defuse the colour(yellow) of the light, creating a warm effect that is pleasing to look at. The unique selling point of the product is that it is multifunctional. It can display motivational quotes, moodlamp, and a great decoration for the table. To create this effects, it is important to know how it works. To turn on the digital clock, simply hover your hand close to the bottom of the product. It uses a ultra-sonic sensor to detect any object in its surrounding. Once it detect an object, 2 things will happen. First it will automatically turn on the LCD to display encouraging quotes, and secondly, the Neo pixel led will light up progressively, getting brighter and brighter as the time goes on. I will reach its brightest in exactly 1 min. After that every thing will turn off and you have to activate it again. Link to video Star Power

Ideation

To start off my project, I use thinginverse to looks for inspiration for my project. I came a across a digital clock/mood lamp/table top decoration. Its minimalistic design accompany by its multi-functional features makes it a great product.

Rough sketch

With minimalistic being the main feature, i decide to make a product that is both stylish and multi-functional. To make it unique, i change a few component; shape and clock function. Instead of a clock function, i changed it to display encouraging messages, to motivate the user. To fit the theme of motivation, i decided to shape the moodlamp into a star, and give it a cute face as part of the design. I choose a star shape, because it symbolise great achievements.

Electronics

For my electronic part, i decided to use an ultra-sonic sensor as my in-put device, and both neo pixel and LCD display as my out-put device. The reasoning behind this is because both the ultra-sonic sensor and LCD adds character to my product, acting as the eyes and mouth. The led is use to create a mood lamp effect.Link to video Star Power (electronics)

Arduino Code

To make my electronics run. I use arduino to code how i want my electronics to perform. Here is a brief run down of how my electronic work from start to finish. First, the ultra-sonic sensor detect any object within 7.5cm, which trigger the led and lcd. The lcd will display encouraging messages, while the led will progressively get brighter, reaching its brightest in 1 min. After 1 min, everything will stop and user have to move an object within range of the ultra-sonic sensor.

Thinker CAD

I use thinker cad to visualize how my circuit will connect and also to check whether it will work. It gives me the freedom to try out many ways of connecting my circuit, and how my circuit would act.

3D Printing

The components that i 3d printed for my product are the casing of the whole product and a Arduino Uno R3 holder. I use inventor to design the components. For the printing machine, I use Creality Ender 3 Pro, as It provide the fastest printing speed and best build quality. For my slicing soft ware, i use the latest version of cura, cura 5.10, which helps speed up the printing time by alot.

2D Laser cutting

The 2D component that i laser cut was the shape of he star. I had to laser cut 2 piece, one is the front which contain all the holes for the ultra-sonic sensor and LCD. We use the Software Corel draw to import our image that we want to laser cut. In the software, we had to learn what are the setting we have to input, depending on the type of cut, and the material. In my case i use 5mm ply wood, and all my cut is vector. So i had to make sure the type of my line is "hairline", next we had to on aim assist and auto focus. Then we have to add in the percentage of power, frequency, cut speed and thickness of the material.
